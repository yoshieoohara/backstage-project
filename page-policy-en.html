privacy policy

Backstage Project Co., Ltd. (hereinafter referred to as "the Company") recognizes the importance of personal information and strives to appropriately manage and protect it based on the following policy:

1. Acquisition of Personal Information
The Company will acquire personal information by lawful and fair means to the extent necessary for the execution of its business operations.

2. Purpose of Use of Personal Information
The acquired personal information will be used for the following purposes:

- Responding to inquiries and requests
- Providing, shipping, and after-sales service of products and services
- Guidance and operation of events and campaigns
- Conducting marketing and surveys
- Recruitment selection and employment management
- Other purposes explicitly indicated at the time of acquisition

3. Management of Personal Information
The Company will maintain personal information accurately and up-to-date, and implement appropriate safety management measures to prevent unauthorized access, loss, destruction, alteration, or leakage.

4. Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties
Except as permitted by law, the Company will not provide personal information to third parties without the consent of the individual.

5. Disclosure, Correction, and Deletion of Personal Information
Based on requests from the individual, the Company will appropriately handle disclosure, correction, deletion, or suspension of use of personal information in accordance with laws and regulations.

6. Compliance with Laws and Continuous Improvement
The Company will comply with laws and other norms related to personal information protection and will review and strive to improve this policy as appropriate.
